About US

Founded by Late Shri Brij Bhushan Tayal in the 1950s, Brij has evolved from a small trading firm into a leading manufacturer of customised uniforms and corporate merchandise. With over 50 years of experience, we operate fully integrated production units in New Delhi and Sonipat, offering complete in-house solutions from design to delivery. Guided by values of Integrity, Teamwork, Responsibility, Growth, and Customer Satisfaction, Brij blends innovation with craftsmanship to deliver premium, sustainable, and customised apparel. We help brands express their identity through creative design, advanced manufacturing, and quality that speaks for itself.
