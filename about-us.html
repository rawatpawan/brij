About Us

Founded by Late Shri Brij Bhushan Tayal in the 1950s, Brij has evolved from a small trading firm into a leading manufacturer of customised uniforms and corporate merchandise. With over 50 years of experience, we operate fully integrated production units in New Delhi and Sonipat, offering complete in-house solutions from design to delivery. Guided by values of Integrity, Teamwork, Responsibility, Growth, and Customer Satisfaction, Brij blends innovation with craftsmanship to deliver premium, sustainable, and customised apparel. We help brands express their identity through creative design, advanced manufacturing, and quality that speaks for itself.

Brij – Where Every Stitch Builds Your Brand.

FOUNDER’S MESSAGE

“Founding this company allowed me an opportunity – an opportunity to build something that can create more opportunities for others. I am proud that we grew as a company over the decades. But if you want to look at the real growth that is beyond the profits and the balance sheets, look at our people. They are at the centre of everything we do. By caring for our employees, their families, and our clients, I have no doubt that our company will continue to be successful.”

PRAVEEN TAYAL

Managing Director & Chairman

Praveen Tayal, son of Lt. Shri Brij Bhushan Tayal, is at the helm of Brij & Co. since the early 1990s, turning it into a market leader in customised apparel merchandise. As India opened its doors to the global economy, Brij & Co. ventured into corporate wear in 1990 to offer its services to the multinational companies entering the Indian market. With a keen interest in Research & Development, Praveen keeps up with the market trends to further his father’s passion and vision. And just like his father, Praveen believes in employing ethical business practices and keeping Brij Customers & Employees Satisfied & motivated, in turn, have made Brij one of the most trusted & preferred brand in India.

Sustaining Relationships.
Sustaining the Environment.

Carrying this legacy forward, Mr. Praveen Tayal has spent 35 years nurturing Brij with resilience, adaptability, and foresight. Under his leadership, Brij has grown into a trusted partner for customised uniforms and apparels — known not only for uncompromising quality but also for innovation that looks ahead, balancing strong relationships with a commitment to sustaining the environment for generations to come.

APOORV TAYAL

Unit Head & Business Development

Apoorv Tayal, a graduate in Fashion & Lifestyle Business Management from Nottingham Trent University,, is Unit head and looks after business development at Brij. With the rapid expansion of the global apparel segment, Apoorv focuses on ways to enhance Brij’s Customer base & and thrive in the new market. He works closely with the top management to understand changing customer preferences.

ARCHIT TAYAL

Head, Process & Systems

Archit Tayal, a graduate in Fashion Business Management from Nottingham Trent University, joined his family business in 2017 as the head of Business Unit & Process. His work centres on ensuring process & system within the organisation along with strengthening the online business model.
